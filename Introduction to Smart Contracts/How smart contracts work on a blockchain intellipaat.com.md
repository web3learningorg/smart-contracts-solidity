### A Revolutionary Concept
Smart contracts were first envisioned by **Nick Szabo in 1994**, long before blockchain existed. Using the vending machine analogy, Szabo showed how trust could be embedded into code. His ideas later inspired **Bitcoin** and the launch of **Ethereum**, which brought programmable contracts to life.  

### Why They Matter
Smart contracts are transforming industries:  
- **Finance (DeFi):** Lending, borrowing, and decentralized exchanges without intermediaries.  
- **Supply Chains:** Real-time tracking and automated payments.  
- **Real Estate & Insurance:** Seamless property transfers and instant claim payouts.  
- **Digital Assets & NFTs:** Automated royalties and ownership verification.
