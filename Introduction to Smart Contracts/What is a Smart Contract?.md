## Smart Contracts Unveiled: How Blockchain Transforms Agreements

Smart contracts are self-executing programs on blockchain networks that automatically enforce agreements when predefined conditions are met.  
They reduce reliance on intermediaries, lower costs, and enhance security and transparency. From Ethereum and DeFi to supply chain, healthcare, real estate, and NFTs, smart contracts are transforming industries by automating transactions and building trustless systems.
