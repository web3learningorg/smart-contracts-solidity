## Nick Szabo's Vision: The Birth of Smart Contracts

Long before blockchain became mainstream, Nick Szabo conceived the idea of smart contracts in 1994. These self-executing digital agreements automate transactions, reduce reliance on intermediaries, and underpin the decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem. From his "Bit Gold" proposal to Ethereum-powered smart contracts, Szabo's visionary concepts laid the foundation for today's Web3 applications and trustless digital systems.
